# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-07-01*

## Product Overview

**One-liner:** AccountIQ helps SME owners get fast, fixed-fee, professionally reviewed business valuation reports from uploaded financial statements.

**What it does:** AccountIQ extracts and normalises financial statement data, uses AI-assisted report generation to prepare a first draft, and routes valuation reports through a human review process before delivery. The first public Commercial MVP focuses on Business Valuation Reports, with forecasting/advisory consultation as the primary upsell.

**Product category:** Business valuation reports, SME financial reporting, financial advisory technology.

**Product type:** SaaS-enabled professional service for the Commercial MVP. Longer term, it may become a broader financial intelligence SaaS and referral network.

**Business model:** Pay-per-report. Initial hero product is a fixed-fee Business Valuation Report. Forecasting/advisory consultation is a manual upsell until separately productised.

## Target Audience

**Target companies:** New Zealand and Australian SMEs that need a credible indicative business valuation, typically for sale readiness, funding conversations, shareholder planning, succession, or strategic decision-making.

**Decision-makers:** Owner-operators, founders, directors, shareholders, and finance/admin managers acting for the business owner.

**Primary use case:** "I need to understand what my business might be worth without starting an expensive, slow advisory engagement."

**Jobs to be done:**

- Get an indicative valuation report from recent financial statements.
- Prepare for a sale, funding discussion, shareholder conversation, or strategic planning session.
- Understand whether forecasting/advisory support is needed before taking the next step.

**Use cases:**

- Owner preparing to sell in the next 6-24 months.
- Founder considering capital raising or debt funding.
- Shareholder needing a valuation reference point.
- Business owner wanting a fast sanity check before paying for deeper advisory work.

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** SME owners often need valuation clarity but face slow, expensive, opaque advisory processes or unreliable DIY estimates.

**Why alternatives fall short:**

- Traditional valuation engagements can be too expensive or slow for early-stage decision-making.
- Online calculators may feel untrustworthy or too generic.
- Accountants may not have a lightweight valuation product ready to offer.
- AI-only tools raise trust, confidentiality, and professional standards concerns.

**What it costs them:** Delayed decisions, weak funding/sale preparation, unclear expectations, and unnecessary advisory spend before they know whether deeper work is justified.

**Emotional tension:** "I need a credible answer, but I do not want to be sold a full advisory engagement before I know where I stand."

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct:** BizVal and fixed-fee business valuation providers. They offer clear pricing and credibility, but may still rely on traditional order forms and slower manual workflows.

**Secondary:** Accountants, corporate finance advisers, valuation specialists. They offer professional judgement, but often with higher cost, longer timelines, and less self-serve convenience.

**Indirect:** Spreadsheet models, business-for-sale estimate tools, online calculators, and generic AI chat. They are fast or cheap, but may lack professional review, defensible methodology, and trust.

## Differentiation

**Key differentiators:**

- Fixed-fee, valuation-first public offer.
- Upload-led workflow using financial statement extraction.
- AI-assisted first draft with human review before delivery.
- Clear in-app report history and paid report access.
- Consultation upsell only where useful, not forced upfront.

**How we do it differently:** AccountIQ combines software speed with an explicit review step. The customer buys a clear report product first, then can opt into deeper advisory help if the valuation raises strategic questions.

**Why that's better:** It lowers friction for the first decision while preserving professional judgement and trust.

**Why customers choose us:** Price certainty, speed, a clear output, and confidence that a person reviews the report before delivery.

## Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Is this just AI guessing? | The software prepares the first draft, but the valuation report is reviewed before delivery. The output is indicative and not financial advice. |
| Can I trust you with my financial statements? | The product needs clear privacy, confidentiality, data-use, and deletion policies before public launch. |
| Is this a formal certified valuation? | No. The Commercial MVP should position the report as indicative unless a separate compliant engagement is explicitly offered. |
| Why pay before seeing the report? | Payment happens only after upload and pre-payment validation. If the report becomes unserviceable after payment, refund/cancellation paths must be defined. |

**Anti-persona:** Customers needing court-standard valuations, regulated financial advice, audited financial statements, complex group restructures, or a certified valuation engagement should be referred to a full professional service.

## Switching Dynamics

**Push:** Existing options feel expensive, slow, unclear, or too informal.

**Pull:** Fixed fee, fast upload flow, clear report output, and professional review.

**Habit:** Business owners ask their accountant by default or avoid the question until a transaction forces it.

**Anxiety:** Confidentiality, AI accuracy, whether the report will be accepted, and whether "indicative" is enough.

## Customer Language

**How they describe the problem:**

- "What is my business worth?"
- "I need a valuation but I do not want a huge advisory bill."
- "Can I get something credible before I talk to buyers, banks, or investors?"
- "I have financial statements but I do not know how to turn them into a valuation."

**How they describe us:**

- "Fixed-fee business valuation report"
- "Upload financials, get a reviewed valuation"
- "A first step before deeper advisory work"

**Words to use:** fixed-fee, indicative valuation, reviewed, business valuation report, upload financials, price certainty, confidential, professional review, optional consultation.

**Words to avoid:** certified, guaranteed, advice, official valuation, instant valuation, AI replaces adviser, court-standard, audit-grade.

**Glossary:**

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Indicative valuation | A valuation estimate/report for decision support, not a regulated or certified valuation engagement unless separately agreed |
| Reviewer | Human professional who reviews generated report output before customer delivery |
| Pre-payment validation | Automated/basic check that uploaded financial statements are readable and likely serviceable before asking for payment |
| Commercial MVP | First public product loop: marketing site, upload, payment, reviewed valuation, delivery, consultation upsell |
