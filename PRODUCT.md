# Product

## Register

product

## Users

SME owners, founders, directors, shareholders, and their advisers who need valuation clarity before a sale, funding conversation, shareholder discussion, or deeper advisory engagement. They are usually handling sensitive financial data, comparing trust signals, and trying to understand whether the service is credible enough to start.

## Product Purpose

AccountIQ is a commercial MVP for fixed-fee SME financial reports, starting with a Business Valuation Report. The public site should make the offer clear and trustworthy; the authenticated app should let customers sign up, upload financial statements, see report status, and return to generated reports. Success is a complete commercial loop: visitor understands the offer, trusts it enough to upload financials, pays only after validation, receives a reviewed report, and can be offered appropriate consultation.

## Brand Personality

Clear, careful, commercially credible. The voice should feel expert without sounding institutional, direct without overclaiming, and calm enough for users handling private financial information.

## Anti-references

Avoid broad SaaS platform language, generic finance stock imagery, abstract AI-first positioning, purple/blue gradient startup tropes, decorative glassmorphism, and claims such as "instant valuation", "certified valuation", "financial advice", "guaranteed valuation", "court-standard", or "AI replaces your adviser". Do not use CAANZ logos, professional credentials, testimonials, or social proof unless permission and entitlement are clear.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with the concrete customer outcome: a fixed-fee indicative business valuation report.
2. Build trust before asking for money or financial documents.
3. Treat compliance boundaries as product clarity, not fine print.
4. Show the workflow and status plainly so customers always know what happens next.
5. Use human review and consultation as credibility and upside, not decorative marketing copy.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA for the public site and authenticated app. Financial-data workflows must be keyboard accessible, readable on mobile, clear under zoom/text scaling, and understandable without relying on color alone. Motion should respect `prefers-reduced-motion`; analytics and tracking should be privacy-safe and consent-aware before public launch.
